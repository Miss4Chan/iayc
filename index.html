What is our platform all about?

After the formation of our Solar System, many rocky fragments were left to orbit the Sun. Although most of them have a stable orbit between Jupiter and Mars, a region known as the Asteroid Belt, there is still a risk of collision with Earth. Such an occurrence can cause moderate to severe damage and even a cataclysmic extinction-level event, like the one that wiped out the dinosaurs about 66 million years ago. Scientists around the globe tirelessly work to discover NEOs (Near Earth Objects) and to classify the apparent risk they can pose to human life. Although currently no high-risk NEOs are known, it is imperative that we prepare for such an event and develop the necessary technology that can be used to modify an asteroid’s trajectory that is supposed to collide with Earth

Our game raises awareness about this issue through interactive quizzes and engaging gameplay. The user is tasked to accelerate a starship in order to avoid the asteroids while learning facts about the necessity to focus scientific efforts towards building better tools to detect and deflect asteroids and other NEOs.
